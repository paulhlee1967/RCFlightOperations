Reports

Eight built-in reports give you a clear picture of your club's membership health, finances, and compliance — all exportable to CSV or PDF.

Overview

All roles can access Reports (Reports in the top nav). Reports are grouped in the left sidebar by category. Select a report, optionally adjust filters (year, membership type), and the data loads immediately — no page reload.

Stat cards above each report give you the headline numbers at a glance before you read the table.

Membership Reports

Membership Snapshot

Your go-to overview report. Shows total members broken down by type (Adult, Youth, Senior, Spouse), renewal status, and flags (life members, free members, inactive). Includes a chart for quick visualization.

Member Type Breakdown

A detailed view of how many members fall into each type. Useful for AMA charter reporting, which often requires member counts by category.

Not Yet Renewed

Lists all members who have a previous year's renewal but have not yet renewed for the selected year. Filter by membership type to focus your outreach. Export this list as CSV to mail or email renewal reminders.

Use Email members (or the Download email CSV instead link on that page) for renewal outreach — both use the same opted-in list. For postal campaigns, export the full CSV from Members → Export and filter or review Allow postal mail in the spreadsheet.

Compliance Reports

AMA/FAA Compliance

Shows members whose AMA or FAA registrations have expired or will expire within 60 days. Split into four sections: AMA expired, AMA expiring soon, FAA expired, FAA expiring soon. Each row links directly to that member's Compliance tab.

Gate Key Compliance

Lists all members who have a gate key number on file. Useful for auditing field access and recovering keys from members who haven't renewed.

Cross-reference Gate Key Compliance with Not Yet Renewed to find members who still have a key but haven't paid for the current year.

Finance Reports

Revenue by Year

Shows total dues collected, broken down by year. Includes dues, initiation fees, late fees, and complementary count. A bar chart makes year-over-year trends easy to spot. Filter by membership type to compare Adult vs. Youth revenue.

Waived / Free Memberships

Lists all members with the "Free membership" or "Life member" flag, plus any complementary renewals recorded in a given year. Useful for board reporting and budget planning.

People Reports

Birthdays This Month

Lists members whose birthday falls in the current calendar month. A fun one for club newsletters and meeting announcements. The callout at the top highlights the count for quick reference.

Export & Email

Every report has export options in the top-right toolbar:

OptionOutputBest for
Export CSV .csv file download Spreadsheet analysis, mail merge, AMA filings
Export PDF .pdf file download Board meeting handouts, printed records
Email report (toolbar → modal) Sends one formatted HTML email to an address you type (stat summary + table) Sending a snapshot to yourself, the treasurer, or the board
Email members (when shown) Opens a compose screen to message each member in the report who has an email and Allow email from the club checked. On that screen you can also use Download email CSV instead for the same recipient list (for mail merge or external tools). Broadcast-style reminders tied to that report (e.g. not yet renewed, birthdays, compliance)
Who gets “email members” and email CSV exports Only members with Allow email from the club enabled on their Contact tab are included. The count on the button reflects that filtered list.
Outbound mail settings Email report and email members use the server’s mail configuration. If messages don’t arrive or land in spam, ask your club’s webmaster to configure SMTP. See Administration → Email Configuration.

Printing Reports

Use your browser's print function (Ctrl+P / ⌘+P). Reports have a built-in print stylesheet that hides the navigation, sidebar, and export buttons — only the title, stat cards, and table will appear on the printed page.
