Reports

Year-over-year membership, revenue, retention, and compliance reports — viewable on screen and exportable to CSV or a branded PDF, with the option to email them.

Overview

The Reports page turns your member and payment records into clear, year-over-year summaries. Every report can be viewed on screen, downloaded as a CSV (for spreadsheets) or a branded PDF (for the board binder), and emailed to one or more addresses.

Reports always count members the same way the rest of the app does, so the numbers match your dashboard. For each past year the app uses a frozen roster — a snapshot of who was a member that year — so historical counts don't change as people renew or lapse later. The current year uses live membership rules.

Using the Reports Page

  1. Open Reports from the top navigation.
  2. Pick a report from the list on the left. The table updates immediately.
  3. For reports that cover a single year, choose the Year from the dropdown at the top right.
  4. Use Download CSV or Download PDF to save a copy, or the Email this report panel below the table to send it.

The Six Reports

Membership by year

Current members for every calendar year, with the change versus the prior year.

ColumnMeaning
YearCalendar / membership year
MembersNumber of members counted for that year
Change vs priorDifference from the year before (green for growth, red for decline)

Retention & churn

How well you keep members from one year to the next.

ColumnMeaning
Prior yearMembers counted the previous year
RetainedMembers present in both years
NewIn this year but not the prior year
LapsedIn the prior year but not this one
Retention rateRetained ÷ prior-year members, as a percentage

Membership type mix

A breakdown of a selected year's members by membership type (e.g. Adult, Youth, Spouse), with each type's share of the total. Disabled or legacy types (such as Senior) still appear if any members hold them. Takes a year.

Not yet renewed

Members who counted last year but haven't renewed for the selected year yet — your follow-up list. Shows name, type, email, and the renewal year currently on file. Takes a year, and supports emailing the group (see Email Members).

See Year Selector & Renewal Season for how this report automatically looks ahead to next year once your renewal drive begins.

Revenue by year

Dues, initiation, and late-fee revenue recorded per membership year, with a grand total.

ColumnMeaning
PaymentsNumber of payment records for the year
DuesTotal annual / prorated dues collected
InitiationTotal initiation fees
Late feesTotal late fees
TotalSum of the three above
Revenue is attributed to the membership year recorded on each payment — so a renewal you collect in October for next year counts toward next year.

AMA/FAA compliance

Current members whose AMA or FAA credentials are already expired or will expire within 60 days, soonest first. Shows AMA and FAA numbers, expiration dates, and whether each is Expired or Expiring. This report is always current-state, so it doesn't take a year.

Year Selector & Renewal Season

Reports that cover a single year (Membership type mix and Not yet renewed) show a Year dropdown. Year-over-year reports don't need one — they always show every year.

Not yet renewed is special. For most of the year it asks "who from last year hasn't renewed for this year?" But once your club's renewal drive begins — by default October 15 — it rolls forward to ask "who from this year hasn't renewed for next year?", matching the renewals your team is actually collecting. The dashboard's "Not yet renewed" card follows the same rule, so the two always agree.

The pre-book start date is configurable under Administration → Installation → Renewal year default. You can always override the year manually with the dropdown.

Data Accuracy Notice

Older clubs often didn't track full membership history before adopting this app — so counts and revenue for early years are reconstructed from whatever payment records exist and may undercount.

To be transparent, reports show a warning notice whenever they include data from before your club's first fully-tracked year. The same caveat travels with the data into the PDF, the emailed copy, and a footnote row in the CSV.

Set your "complete data" year. Under Administration → Installation → Reports — complete data starting year, enter the first year your records are trustworthy (default 2027). Reports flag earlier years automatically; once a year is at or after this threshold, the notice disappears.

Download CSV & PDF

Both buttons sit at the top right of every report.

FormatBest forNotes
CSV Spreadsheets (Excel, Google Sheets), further analysis Includes column headers, totals, and any data-accuracy footnote.
PDF Board meetings, printed records, sharing a tidy snapshot Branded with your club logo and colors, with page numbers in the footer.
The PDF automatically uses your club logo and theme colors from Administration → Configuration. If PDF export isn't available on your host, the page lets you know and CSV still works.

Email a Report

Below each report is an Email this report panel. Use it to send the report — table and all — to one or more people, such as your board or treasurer.

  1. Enter one or more addresses in Send to, separated by commas.
  2. Optionally add a short note to appear above the table.
  3. Click Send.

The email arrives styled in your club's branding (logo, colors), with the report table and any data-accuracy notice included.

Email Members (Cohort)

The Not yet renewed report adds a second panel: Email these members — a personalized reminder sent to everyone on the list at once. Handy for a renewal nudge.

  1. Open Not yet renewed and confirm the year is correct.
  2. In the Email these members panel, write a subject and message.
  3. Use these tokens to personalize each message:
    • {first_name} — the member's first name
    • {last_name} — the member's last name
    • {club_name} — your club's name
  4. Click Send. Each member gets their own copy in your club's branding.
Only opted-in members are contacted. Messages go only to members who have an email address and have "Allow email" turned on (see Members → Email & postal). Anyone who has opted out is skipped automatically. This feature is available to Editors and Treasurers.

FAQ

Why don't my older years match what I remember?

Years before your club started tracking full history are reconstructed from payment records, so they can undercount. That's exactly what the data-accuracy notice is warning about. Going forward, each year is captured as a frozen roster and stays accurate.

The PDF button says export isn't available.

PDF generation needs a library that may not be installed on every host. CSV always works; ask your webmaster to enable PDF export if you need it (see Installation).

I emailed the "not yet renewed" group but some members didn't get it.

Only members with an email address and "Allow email" enabled are contacted; everyone else is skipped on purpose. Check the member's contact preferences.

Do reports move money or charge anyone?

No. Like the rest of the app, reports only summarize records you've already entered — RC Flight Operations never processes payments. See About Payments.
