App Overview

A plain-English tour of what RC Flight Operations does and how to find your way around.

What is RC Flight Operations?

RC Flight Operations is a membership management tool built specifically for AMA-affiliated RC flying clubs. It gives your club secretary, membership coordinator, and board members one organized place to handle everything membership-related — without spreadsheets, paper logs, or pieced-together systems.

Here's what it handles in one place:

  • Member records — contact info, photo, one phone, one mailing address, and emergency contact
  • New member wizard — guided five-step signup from first contact through payment, badge printing, and mailing
  • AMA & FAA compliance — track numbers, expiration dates, and verify AMA membership with one click
  • Dues & renewals — record on-time, late, prorated, and complimentary renewals; handle life members and special cases
  • CR80 ID badge design & printing — design multiple named card templates, preview with real member data, then print personalized badges
  • Reports — membership snapshots, retention, revenue, compliance, incidents, birthdays, and data-quality lists
  • CSV import & export — bring in your existing member list and export it in formats for AMA filings, newsletters, and backups
  • Safety incident log — keep a dated record of field incidents for your club's safety records
  • Multiple users with roles — give board members access at the right level without sharing a single login
Built for clubs like yours RC Flight Operations is designed around how AMA-chartered clubs actually work — annual renewals, compliance checks before flying, ID badge printing at renewal time, and a small volunteer team doing the work.

A Note on Payments

RC Flight Operations does not process payments of any kind. It has no connection to credit card processors, PayPal, bank accounts, or any other payment system. There is no way to pay dues through this app.

What it does do is let you record payments that you've already received through your club's normal channels — whether that's cash at a meeting, a check in the mail, or a transfer through a service your club already uses. Once you've collected dues by whatever method works for your club, you log the payment here so your records stay accurate and members show up as renewed.

Think of it like a ledger. Money changes hands offline; you write it down here. Reports and renewal status are based entirely on what you've recorded — so keep your records current and they'll stay accurate.

Look & Feel

RC Flight Operations uses a consistent layout across the app: club-themed navbar and buttons, card-based pages, and a shared sidebar tool panel on complex screens (badge designer, reports, club configuration). Your club's colors, logo, and favicon are set under Administration → Configuration → Design and apply everywhere — including the in-app Help & Documentation pages when you are logged in.

Status badges (current, suspended, life member, etc.) use earth-tone colors that harmonize with the default palette but remain readable when you customize your theme.

User Roles at a Glance

Each person who logs in has a role that controls what they can see and do. Assigning roles thoughtfully means Club Staff can run renewals and exports without editing member contact records, and a board member with Report Viewer access can review aggregates without browsing individual member details.

RoleBest forWhat they can do
Administrator Club secretary, webmaster Full access — members, renewals, reports, badge design, users, and club configuration
Membership Manager Membership coordinator Add and edit members, record renewals, run reports, design and print badges — everything except managing users or changing club settings
Club Staff Signup desk, renewal volunteers Record renewals, approve applications, view and export member lists, print badges, run reports — cannot edit member contact details
Report Viewer Board member needing oversight View and export reports and read the incident log — no member list, exports, or operational actions

→ Full details on what each role can and can't do: Administration — Users & Roles.

One Club Per Installation

RC Flight Operations is built for a single club per database: your members, users, badge design, and settings all belong to that one organization. There is no shared hosting of multiple separate clubs in one app instance.

If you need completely separate clubs, run a separate copy of the app with its own database and config.php (or separate virtual host / subdomain per club).
