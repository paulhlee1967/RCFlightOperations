RC Flight Operations

Free & Open Source

Membership management software built for AMA-affiliated RC flying clubs — by people who understand how clubs actually work.

What It Does

RC Flight Operations keeps your club's membership organized so your secretary and membership coordinator spend less time hunting through spreadsheets and more time enjoying the hobby. Everything your club needs is in one place:

  • Member records with contact info, photos, phones, and addresses
  • AMA and FAA compliance tracking with live AMA membership verification
  • Renewal recording — on-time, late, prorated, and complimentary
  • CR80 ID card design and printing (drag-and-drop badge designer)
  • Eight built-in reports with CSV and PDF export
  • CSV import from your existing spreadsheet or old system
  • Role-based access — Admin, Editor, Treasurer, Viewer
  • Field safety incident log for club records
  • Fully customizable to your club's name, logo, and colors

About Payments

RC Flight Operations does not process payments. It has no connection to credit cards, PayPal, bank accounts, or any payment service. No money moves through this system.

What it does is let you record dues payments you've already collected — whether your members pay at the field, mail a check, or use whatever method your club prefers. Once you've received dues through your normal process, you log it here to keep member records accurate and up to date.

Think of it as your membership ledger. Money in, you write it down. Reports and renewal status reflect exactly what's been recorded.
