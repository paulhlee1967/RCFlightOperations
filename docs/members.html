Members

Everything about adding, editing, and managing your club's member records.

Member List

Go to Members in the top nav. You'll see a paginated list of all members with quick-glance info: name, membership type, renewal year, AMA status, and badge print status.

From the list you can:

  • Click any member's name to open their full record
  • Click Renew to go straight to the renewal workflow
  • Click Print card to print their ID badge
  • Use the Export CSV dropdown to download the list

Search & Filter

The filter bar above the list lets you narrow members by:

  • Status chips: All, Active, Not renewed, Inactive, Suspended
  • Membership type: Adult, Youth, Senior, Spouse
  • Search box: Searches first name, last name, and email in real time (press Enter or click Search)
Filters are additive — you can combine status + type + search to get exactly the list you need. The URL updates as you filter, so you can bookmark a particular view.

Adding a Member

  1. Click New member (top right of the Members page).
  2. Fill in the Contact tab — at minimum, first and last name.
  3. Switch to Compliance and enter their AMA number and expiration date.
  4. Switch to Membership and set their type (Adult, Youth, Senior, Spouse) and date joined.
  5. Click Save member.
  6. Once saved, go to Renew to record their first dues payment.
Save before renewing The member record must be saved before you can record a payment. The Renew button appears on the member record after the first save.

Email & postal preferences

On the Contact tab, two checkboxes control how your club may use this member’s contact information for routine outreach. Both default to on for new members.

SettingWhat it controls
Allow email from the club When checked, the member may receive club emails, including report-based “email members” messages, scheduled expiry reminders (if your host runs them), and similar automated messages. When unchecked, the app excludes them from those sends and from the “Email only” CSV export.
Allow postal mail When unchecked, the member has opted out of postal mailings (newsletters, renewal packets, etc.). Envelope and mailing letter views still show the address so you can send something required by law or policy, but they display a clear postal opt-out notice so you can confirm before mailing.
These are preferences stored on the member record. They do not replace your club’s own policies or consent processes — they help officers use the app in line with what each member agreed to.

Payment History Tab

A read-only log of every payment recorded for this member. Columns: date paid, membership year, dues amount, initiation fee, late fee, and whether it was a complementary (comp) payment.

To add a payment, use the Renew workflow rather than editing this tab directly. See Renewals & Dues.

Status Flags Explained

A member's status is shown as a badge in the list and on their record. Here's what each one means:

Status shownWhat it means
Active Renewed for the current year, and not marked inactive or suspended
Not renewed Had a renewal in a previous year but has not yet renewed for the current year
Inactive Marked inactive on the Membership tab — kept in the system but excluded from active counts
Suspended Marked suspended on the Membership tab — renewals cannot be recorded until this is cleared
Life Club life member — shown in addition to Active or Not renewed
Free Free or complimentary membership — shown in addition to other status

Member Photo

Upload a headshot on the Contact tab. Supported formats: JPEG, PNG, GIF. Photos are stored securely and only used for badges and internal display.

The photo appears on the badge front when your club's badge template includes a photo element. It does not appear anywhere else in the app.

Deleting a Member

Deletion is permanent Deleting a member removes all their data — contact info, payments, and photos. Consider marking them Inactive instead to preserve history.

To delete: open the member's record, scroll to the bottom, and click Delete member. You'll be asked to confirm. Admin and Editor roles only.
